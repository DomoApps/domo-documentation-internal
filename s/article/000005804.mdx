### Intro

<Warning>**Warning:** This topic is for INTERNAL USE ONLY. Do NOT share any of the content in this page with customers.</Warning>

Domo Support occasionally gets customer requests related to the following products:

- Corda PopChart version 7
- DomoTools version 8
- Centerview versions 4 or 5

These are legacy tools and requests are not currently handled by Domo Support. Instead, requests are sent directly to an engineer responsible for their upkeep. The process to follow is described below.

---

### Pass Legacy Tools Request to Engineer

Follow these steps if a request relating to one of the tools listed above is received by Domo Support:

1. Set the case category to one of the following:
   - LEGACY > Corda PopChart
   - LEGACY > Domo Tools
   - LEGACY > CenterView
2. Thank the customer for reaching out and advise them that their case is being transferred to an individual to review their request (see [template](#template_for_case_transfer) below).
3. Change ownership of the case to Mardell Cheney.

   
<Note>**Note:** All billing, renewal, sales, and general accounts questions should go to Mark Christensen ([Mark.Christensen@domo.com](mailto:Mark.Christensen@domo.com)</Note>

    ). The case should still be assigned to Mardell Cheney, but take notes and include a copy of your email to Mark in the case as a private comment.
4. Check the box labeled **Send Notification Email** to advise Mardell of the new case.

Mardell will follow up with the customer and close out the case when appropriate.

### Template for Case Transfer

*You can use this template when advising a customer that their request is being passed on.*

Thank you for reaching out to us regarding [*Product Name*]. We appreciate your continued interest in this product.

Please note that your case has been transferred to our specialized team handling [*Product Name*] inquiries. One of our knowledgeable representatives will follow up with shortly to address your concerns and provide assistance. We understand the important of timely support, and our team is committed to resolving your inquiry promptly and efficiently.

If you have any urgent matters or additional questions in the meantime, please reply to this email, and we will ensure it receives prompt attention. Thank you for your patience and and understanding.
