---
title: "Accessing a Customer Instance"
---

<Warning>**Important:** This topic is for INTERNAL USE ONLY. Do NOT share any of the content in this page with customers!</Warning>

Accessing a customer production instance requires customer permission!

There are two circumstances in which Domo personnel may access a customer production instance:

- A customer might invite a Domo employee into their instance for activity required by the customer, or
- If a customer has an issue, incident or bug, requiring more investigation, it is documented in a support ticket by the Technical Advisor in Support, allowing the Domo employee to enter and investigate the customer production instance.

Access to a customer production instance is only available to those with a User ID in the system or through the Domo Admin Console. Only Support and Engineering have access to the Admin Console without further permission.

If accessing a customer production instance via a support ticket, the process is as follows:

1. Access the Domo Admin Console through SSO.
2. Click on **Production** in the **Areas** section.
3. Choose the appropriate environment (i.e. Prod1, Prod2, etc.).
4. Click on **Customers** in the top bar.
5. Enter the customer instance name in the search field.
6. Check the box next to the desired instance.
7. Click on **Access** under **Operations**.
8. Click on **Start Proxy Session**.
9. Enter a Salesforce.com or Jira ticket number in the **Reason for proxy** field.
10. Start the session.  
     Note that the customer will see this access in their activity log!

Once the incident is closed via support ticket, access ceases for the Domo employee.
