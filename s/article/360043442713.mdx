## To Enable Writeback Connectors

1. Create an internal note on the Case using the following template:

> Connector Engineering Team
>
> This request came through to have the \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ Writeback Connector enabled. Please enable in this instance: \_\_\_\_\_\_.domo.com, and respond to the contact on this case that it has been completed.

2. Assign the Case to the queue: Engineering - Connector Support
