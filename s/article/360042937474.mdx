### L1 Process

The following image  shows the process for all issues relating to Beta features. Following this process will ensure that all Beta issues are handled in the quickest manner possible.

Examples of messaging you can provide to the customer for each path:

**Beta Enhancement**

- Thank you for your feedback. Your suggestion has been forwarded to the development team.

**Beta Bug**

- Your feedback is important to us. We are following up with the development team about your issue.
- The development team is working to address this issue.

**FYI/Feel Good**

- Thank you for your feedback. Your email has been forwarded to the development team.

### L2 Process

- Create a Jira briefly describing the issue, as a bug or enhancement (whichever it is)
- Assign it to the 'betafeedback' Jira user as the ticket owner
- Set the reporter as yourself
- Inform the customer that the Beta Feedback Team will get in touch with them if needed, thank them for participating in the beta, and that you will be closing the ticket from support's side
