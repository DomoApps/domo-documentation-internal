---
title: "Resending the Domo Invite Email"
excerpt: "This article explains how to resend the Domo invite email to users in a Domo instance."
---

<Warning>**Important:** This topic is for INTERNAL USE ONLY. Do NOT share any of the content in this page with customers.</Warning>

In the event that you must resend the Domo invite email (“You got Domo’d”), do the following at any time:

1. Check the box next to the customer in the Admin Console.
2. Under **Operations > Users**, click **Resend Invite E-mail**.
3. Add the users you wish to resend the invite email to.
4. Click **Resend E-mail**.

You can also use this method to search for users in the instance to see if they exist.
